OUR TEAM

How do we work?

We work with partners to identify critical issues in the public service arena surrounding data analytics, labour efficiency, procurement, policy matters etc.

We then begin exploration of the subject matter to determine whether disruptive technologies can be utilized to solve the issue at hand, followed by refinement of solutions and tests.

The next step may involve the development of prototypes that can take form of an application programming interface (API), data repository or data analysis tool, to showcase the "art of the possible".

A final practice of our initiatives involve a lessons learned component encompassing a retrospective review identifying successes, challenges, and a future trajectory.
