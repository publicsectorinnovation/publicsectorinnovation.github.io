Rules as Code

Support rule makers in drafting better rules by creating a machine understandable version of the rules in computer code. This will support testing the effects of rules in real time, identify gaps between a rule's intent and how it is implemented, support more consistent interpretation of rules, reduce costs of compliance and offer new possibilities to improve service delivery.
